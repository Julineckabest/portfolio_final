Process Overview

01

Discovery

Stakeholder workshops, competitive analysis, and brand positioning exercises to define core attributes and target audience.

02

Concept Development

Created three distinct visual directions exploring different approaches to energy, movement, and community themes.

03

Refinement

Developed selected direction into comprehensive system with logo variations, color palette, typography, and pattern library.

04

Application

Designed touchpoints across all channels and created brand guidelines document for internal teams and vendors.

Impact

The rebrand successfully repositioned Pulse as a premium, design-forward fitness brand. Membership inquiries increased 43% in the first quarter post-launch, and social media engagement doubled.

"The new identity perfectly captures our energy and vision. Our members love it, and we've seen a significant uptick in brand recognition."

— Sarah Chen, Pulse Fitness CEO
