About me

Hi, I'm Julie

I'm Julie Jancickova, an Interactive Designer with a passion for crafting engaging digital experiences. With a background in UX design and game development, I specialize in creating user-centered solutions that blend creativity with functionality. My work spans across various industries, including games, healthcare, education, and entertainment, where I strive to make technology more intuitive and enjoyable for users. Currently based in Prague & Edinburgh, working remotely with teams worldwide.

The story

I was always the creative one. Drawing, sewing, getting lost in art galleries, sketching ideas on my tablet. I started my journey after completing my qualifications in Economics and Transportation in 2019. I quickly began working in Telecommunications as an Administration Specialist in a small family business in Prague. I loved the variety of my work, working with clients and solving problems, and cooperating with marketing teams. After a few years, I decided to move abroad to improve my English and gain new experiences. I moved to the Netherlands and started work as an au pair and later on as a machine operator.

My goal was to improve my communication skills, and I decided to move to an English-speaking country‚Äîthe United Kingdom. First, I moved to London, where I stayed for one year, and later moved to Edinburgh.

I started learning by stepping out of my comfort zone and working in customer-facing roles‚Äîfrom hospitality to call centres. I learned how to work under pressure, communicate with a variety of customers, and understand different cultures.
